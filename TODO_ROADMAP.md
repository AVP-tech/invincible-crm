# Future Roadmap

## Next product expansions
1. SaaS subscription tracking and waste alerts for agency and startup finance visibility.
2. Deeper accounting integrations such as QuickBooks, Xero, and invoice status push/pull beyond local invoice tracking.
3. Richer email and WhatsApp sync, including outbound sends, reply threading, and attachment handling.
4. A stronger automation builder with conditions, templates, delays, and guardrails.
5. Workspace switcher plus broader multi-workspace support beyond the current primary-workspace assumption.
6. Mobile app for field-friendly note capture, reminders, and conversation review.
7. Customer health, analytics, and conversion reporting once daily usage is stable.

## Technical expansion hooks
- Multi-tenant workspace model and membership tables.
- Integration event bus for external sync jobs.
- Background processing for reminders, enrichment, and transcript imports.
- Audit trails and permission boundaries for teams.
